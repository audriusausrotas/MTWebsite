# Why Choose Diamond Type Fence, Also Called Rhombus Fence?

"So many plot enclosure solutions, and which fence type suits my case best?" – this question often arises for people looking for a reliable and aesthetic solution.  
One of the most universal and rapidly gaining popularity choices is **Diamond type fence**, also called **Rhombus fence**. This is a closed-type structure characterized by strength, durability and extremely simple maintenance.

Diamond (Rhombus) fence stands out for being twice as strong as Louvre type fence, while the price difference between them is minimal. For this reason, it becomes an excellent choice for both private properties and company territories.

<!--more-->

---

## Rhombus Fence Characteristics

Rhombus fence is made of high-quality German **ThyssenKrupp AG** steel, which is given even a **45-year warranty**.  
The steel is:

- galvanized,
- powder-coated,
- resistant to environmental impact,
- maintains original appearance for a long time.

Color selection is wide – you can choose from the entire **RAL palette**, so the fence is easily matched to buildings and environment.

### **Unique Diamond Fence Construction**

Diamond fence's uniqueness lies in its bent panels:

- fence panels are bent into a **solid rhombus-shaped tube**,
- the inner side becomes **completely invisible**,
- the structure looks **massive and extremely strong**,
- the fence looks **equally aesthetic** from both sides.

Fence panels can be of various widths, allowing creation of individual design adapted to plot architecture.

### **Adjustable Opacity**

Rhombus fence allows regulating gaps between fence panels.  
You can choose:

- greater visibility,
- or almost complete privacy.

Usually Rhombus fence has **5–15 % opacity**, so it is practically non-transparent.

---

## Modern and Minimalist Design

Rhombus fence is characterized by contemporary, minimalist design.  
It has no excessive forms or details, therefore it:

- looks tidy,
- gives the territory modernity,
- matches both new construction and older buildings,
- does not create visual noise.

This is an excellent choice for those who value aesthetics and clean lines.

---

## Rhombus Fence Applicability

Rhombus fence is extremely universal and suitable for various situations.

### **For Private Yards**

- almost non-transparent,
- protects from outside glances,
- gives sense of security to the family.

### **For Company Territories**

- ensures privacy,
- looks solid and tidy,
- perfect for representative zones.

### **For Plots Near Busy Streets**

- retains part of the dust,
- reduces noise spread,
- helps create a quieter environment.

### **For Families with Pets**

- pets are not distracted by passers-by or cars,
- the fence provides a safe, enclosed space.

## Why Choose Exactly Diamond (Rhombus) Fence?

- **Twice as strong** as Louvre type fence.
- **Durable** – up to 45-year warranty.
- **Modern and aesthetic** from both sides.
- **Adjustable opacity** according to needs.
- **Suitable for both private and commercial plots**.
- **Resistant to environmental impact** and easy to maintain.

If you are looking for a fence that is strong, durable, aesthetic and provides maximum privacy, **Diamond (Rhombus) type fence** is one of the best choices in today's market.
