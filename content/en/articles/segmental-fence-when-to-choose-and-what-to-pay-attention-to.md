# Segmental Fence: When to Choose It and What to Pay Attention To?

Today the market offers many different fence types, so everyone can find a solution according to their needs.  
For those seeking the best price-quality ratio, one of the most popular choices is **segmental fence**. It suits both private and commercial territories, and its purpose can differ depending on the object.

Segmental fence is an economical and practical solution. Installation is simple, requiring no special tools, so even the client can easily install it themselves. The kit consists of posts, segments, clamps and bolts – everything is clear and simple.

<!--more-->

---

## When is Segmental Fence the Best Choice?

Segmental fence is especially suitable when you need to enclose a large territory at an affordable price. It is often chosen:

### **1. For Agricultural Purpose Territories**

Segments help protect the plot from:

- rabbits,
- deer,
- other wild animals that can damage plants or crops.

For this reason, segmental fence is popular in homesteads located in the countryside.

### **2. For Domestic Animal Protection**

Dog or bird owners value segmental fence for:

- safety,
- visibility,
- ability to choose appropriate height.

### **3. For Commercial and Public Territories**

Segmental fence is often used for:

- schools,
- kindergartens,
- sports grounds,
- dog walking areas,
- industrial territories.

Why? Because it:

- requires no additional maintenance,
- is durable,
- economical,
- quickly installed.

---

## Why is Segmental Fence So Popular?

The main reason – **excellent price-quality ratio**.  
But this is only part of the advantages.

### **Adjustable Height**

Segmental fence can be:

- low (up to 1 m) – for decorative solutions,
- high (2 m and more) – for greater security, especially with large breed dogs.

### **Easy Installation**

The fence is installed in a few days, often 1–2 assistants are enough.  
This is one of the simplest fence types in terms of installation.

---

## What to Pay Attention to When Choosing Segmental Fence?

For the fence to serve long and maintain aesthetics, it's important to evaluate several criteria.

### **1. Painting Method**

It's recommended to choose **powder coating**, because it:

- is resistant to snow, frost, wind, UV rays,
- does not peel or deform,
- ensures durability.

### **2. Price and Quality**

Although segmental fences may look identical, differences lie in details.  
Just a **5–10% price change** can mean:

- thinner post,
- poorer zinc layer,
- weaker fastening parts,
- less corrosion resistance.

Therefore, it's necessary to check:

- post thickness,
- coating quality,
- zinc layer thickness,
- kit completeness.

---

Segmental fence is a practical, economical and universal solution suitable for both private and commercial territories.  
When properly chosen and quality criteria evaluated, it will serve long and reliably.
