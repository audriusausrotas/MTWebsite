# Minimum Order Value

The minimum order value is **10 EUR**, excluding delivery fees.  
If the cart value is lower – the order is not processed.

---

# Delivery / Pickup Options

Ordered products can be:

- delivered to the address specified by the customer;
- picked up from the seller’s warehouses.

If the selected warehouse has sufficient stock – pickup is **free**.  
If products must be transported from another warehouse – the pickup fee equals the home delivery fee.

The list of MB “Moderni Tvora” warehouses is available in the **“Contacts”** section.

---

# Delivery to the Specified Address

• Products are delivered by a transport company at the customer’s expense.  
• The exact delivery price depends on weight, size, and value – confirmed before order approval.  
• The customer must provide an accurate delivery address.  
• Products are delivered within the timeframes specified in product descriptions.  
• The longest delivery time applies when ordering multiple items.  
• The customer is informed by email, phone, or SMS.  
• Items may arrive separately if shipped from different warehouses.  
• Upon delivery, the customer must inspect the package with the courier.  
• If the package is damaged, the customer may refuse the shipment.  
• A valid ID must be presented when receiving the goods.  
• The customer must check the goods (quantity, quality, completeness) within 3 days.  
 If no claims are submitted – the goods are considered compliant.

---

# Carrying and Loading Services

• Loading services must be ordered separately before payment.  
• The customer pays for loading services.

---

# Pickup at the Seller’s Warehouse

• Products ordered online can be picked up at MB “Moderni Tvora” warehouses.  
• If the warehouse has sufficient stock – pickup is free.  
• If products must be transported from another warehouse – a fee equal to home delivery applies.  
• Once products are ready, the customer is informed by email, phone, or SMS.  
• Products must be picked up:

- within 7 days if paid in advance,
- within 3 days if paying upon pickup.  
  • Failure to pick up within the deadline may result in order cancellation.  
  • When picking up, the customer must:
- provide the order number,
- present the invoice (if issued),
- present a valid ID.  
  • Only the customer may pick up the goods.  
   If another person will pick them up, they must be listed as the recipient.  
   For legal entities, authorization may be required.  
  • Warehouse list is available in the **“Contacts”** section.

# Payment

Customers can pay for products purchased at www.modernitvora.lt via:

**Bank transfer** – payment at any Lithuanian bank.  
**Online banking** – through major Lithuanian banks.

A secure SSL connection is used, ensuring safe transactions.

For questions: **info@modernitvora.lt**
