The Client – the customer for whom the fence equipment is manufactured – hereinafter referred to as the Product.  
The Contractor – MB “Moderni tvora”, which undertakes to manufacture and deliver the Product to the Client.

# 1. SERVICE TERMS AND DESCRIPTION

1.1. The specification and description of the ordered Product are provided in the individual Commercial Offer prepared by the Contractor based on the Client’s request. The Contractor undertakes to manufacture and deliver the Product to the Client’s ownership within the deadline and at the price specified in the Commercial Offer.

1.2. By confirming the Commercial Offer, the Client declares that they have reviewed the characteristics, properties, and service conditions of the Product, which are provided on the website and in the Commercial Offer. The Product is manufactured individually according to the Client’s measurements, taking into account production and installation specifics, and cannot be returned to the Contractor.

1.3. Production deadlines are indicated in the Commercial Offer and are calculated from the date of the advance payment. The advance amount is specified in the Commercial Offer. Payment of the advance also confirms that the Client has reviewed and agrees with the offered service and price.

1.4. The Client must pay the remaining amount before collecting the Product from the Contractor’s warehouse (Bardauskų g. 18, Bardauskai) or before the Product is delivered to the Client’s specified address (an additional service).

1.5. Ownership rights to the Product transfer to the Client only after full payment. If the full amount specified in the Commercial Offer is not paid, the fence equipment remains the property of the Contractor.

# 2. CONTRACTOR’S OBLIGATIONS

2.1. To manufacture the Product within the deadline specified in the Commercial Offer according to the provided list and specification.

2.2. To deliver the Product to the Client’s specified address (additional service). Unloading work is not included unless agreed otherwise.

2.3. The Product is considered delivered when the Client confirms receipt on the delivery note or another document. The Client must carefully inspect the Product (items, quality, etc.) according to the attached specification. Any remarks regarding visible defects must be submitted in writing at the time of signing the delivery document. If no claims are received, the Product is considered to be of proper quality.

2.4. The Contractor is not responsible for deterioration of the Product’s quality if the Client (or third parties) used it for purposes other than intended, failed to follow instructions, violated usage rules, or if visible defects were not recorded in writing at the time of delivery.

2.5. The Contractor agrees to store the finished fence equipment free of charge for up to 14 calendar days from the date of notifying the Client about completion. After this period, storage fees apply. For orders exceeding one EUR pallet size (120×80 cm), a fee of 2.0 EUR (excluding VAT) per day per pallet is applied.

# 3. WARRANTY

3.1. The Product is covered by a 24‑month warranty against hidden manufacturing defects, starting from the invoice date. The warranty covers the cost of replacement parts and labor.

3.2. The warranty does not apply if:

3.2.1. The deterioration or damage is caused by the Client or third parties, improper use, or failure to follow instructions.

3.2.2. The deterioration or damage is caused by force majeure circumstances. Color changes or lime stains on concrete products due to atmospheric effects are natural and not considered hidden defects.

# 4. LIABILITY AND GENERAL REQUIREMENTS

4.1. If the Client unilaterally terminates the order, they must compensate the Contractor for incurred losses and pay a penalty for violating these terms. The penalty equals the advance amount specified in the Commercial Offer. Losses equal the actual damages incurred by the Contractor but not less than 30% of the total order value.

4.2. If the Client fails to pay the invoice on time, they must pay 0.02% late interest per working day based on the total order value.

4.3. If the Contractor fails to deliver the Product on time, they must pay the Client 0.02% late interest per working day based on the total order value.

4.4. Force majeure. The Contractor is not liable for full or partial non‑performance of obligations due to force majeure circumstances. The Contractor must notify the Client in writing within 15 calendar days and return the advance payment.

4.5. The Contractor undertakes to maintain confidentiality and not disclose any commercial, business, or financial information obtained during cooperation.

4.6. Any changes or additions to these Terms are valid only when a Service Agreement is signed. Matters not covered in these Terms or the Agreement are governed by the laws of the Republic of Lithuania.

4.7. All notices and correspondence are delivered to the Client/Contractor in person or sent by mail or email. Both parties acknowledge that email notices have the same legal force as signed documents if sent from the email addresses specified in the order.
