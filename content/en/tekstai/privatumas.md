# Privacy Policy

This Privacy and Cookie Policy (hereinafter – the Policy) applies when you visit the MB “Moderni tvora” website. This Policy does not apply when you browse other companies’ websites accessed through the MB “Moderni tvora” website.

We collect data from visitors to our website in accordance with the Law on Legal Protection of Personal Data of the Republic of Lithuania, the Law on Electronic Communications of the Republic of Lithuania, the European Union data protection laws, other applicable legal acts, and the instructions of supervisory authorities.

---

## Who We Are

MB “Moderni tvora”.  
Website address: https://www.modernitvora.lt  
For any questions, please contact: **info@modernitvora.lt**

---

## Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing Personal Data

a. Your personal data will be used to carry out pre-contractual measures such as preparing offers and performing contracts, in accordance with Article 6(1)(b) of the GDPR.

b. Your personal data will also be processed when there is a legal obligation to do so, in accordance with Article 6(1)(c) of the GDPR (commercial and tax law requirements).

c. Company data may be transferred to a credit agency based on legitimate interests. In the case of a partnership, this may involve the transfer of personal data under Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR to reduce credit risk and ensure financial security.

d. Under Article 26 of the GDPR, your contact and company data may also be shared with MB “Moderni tvora” partner companies. These partners collaborate closely within a CRM system and jointly determine how data is processed at different stages. The institution listed as No. 1 is responsible for collecting your data for the purposes described in points a–c. All partners act as joint controllers and share responsibilities accordingly.

---

## Data Retention Period

We store your data only for as long as necessary to achieve the relevant processing purpose. Once the retention period expires, the data is deleted. This includes retention periods required by commercial and tax laws.

## What Personal Data We Collect

### Comments

- Data entered in the comment form
- Visitor’s IP address
- Browser user agent string
- A hashed version of your email may be sent to Gravatar to check if you use the service.

### Files

If you upload images, avoid including EXIF GPS location data, as visitors may extract this information.

## Cookies

When you leave a comment, you may choose to save your name, email, and website in cookies for convenience.  
Cookie durations:

- Comment cookies: **1 year**
- Login cookies: **2 days**
- Display settings cookies: **1 year**
- “Remember me”: **2 weeks**

---

## Embedded Content from Other Websites

Embedded content (videos, images, articles, etc.) behaves the same as if you visited the source website.  
These websites may:

- collect data about you,
- use cookies,
- track your interactions with embedded content.

## With Whom We Share Your Data

Your personal data may be shared with suppliers if your order includes home delivery. If you decline this service, your data will not be shared.

## How Long We Store Your Data

- User account data is stored as long as the account exists.
- Order information is stored for the period required by tax and accounting laws.
